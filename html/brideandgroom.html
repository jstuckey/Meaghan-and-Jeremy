Bride and Groom

Our Story

Meaghan and Jeremy met on February 18, 2006 while hanging out with mutual friends in a cluttered, dimly lit, dorm room of Chestertown Hall at the University of Maryland College Park. Reluctantly forced to be partners for the night's activities, they engaged in affable yet mundane conversation throughout the evening. Despite the less-than-idyllic circumstances of their first encounter, Meaghan piqued Jeremy's interest enough for him to chase after her as she was leaving to ask for her name. In neighboring Bel Air Hall, Meaghan later attempted to locate Jeremy on Facebook in vain, mistakenly searching for his nickname: Jeremiah. With proper guidance from their friend Schade, Meaghan eventually found Jeremy and asked to meet for breakfast the following morning. Jeremy, having been briefed by Schade, was awaiting Meaghan's message and agreed to attend.

After that breakfast at the Silver Diner, Meaghan and Jeremy saw each other a few more times, including a viewing of the Vagina Monologues chaperoned by Meaghan's friend Laura. On the evening of March 4, 2006, Meaghan came over to Jeremy's room in Baltimore Hall. Jeremy sat taciturn and nervous while Tiny Dancer by Elton John played in the background, working up the gumption to ask Meaghan out. When Jeremy finally set his nerves aside and asked, Meaghan immediately accepted, dispelling the awkward tension that need not have existed in the first place. From there, they walked across campus back to Chestertown Hall, this time as boyfriend and girlfriend.

Then finally the day came where Jeremy was ready to propose. Due to Meaghan's nosey deportment and proclivity to ruin her own surprises, Jeremy went ring shopping in secrecy. Only Arnie the jeweler was privy to his plan. On the picturesque afternoon of May the nineteenth, Jeremy took Meagahan to the University of Maryland under the guise of a campus stroll and the proposition of D.P. Dough calzones for dinner. With ring in cargo pocket, Jeremy led Meaghan to Baltimore Hall where their journey began six years prior. Equally as nervous, Jeremy led Meaghan around back of the building, in view of his old dorm room and safely away from the prying eyes of vacating college students. It was here that became the site of their betrothal.

Meaghan and Jeremy will be wed on July 20, 2013, a culmination of their seven-plus year journey together.
