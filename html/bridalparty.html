Bryan Stuckey, Best Man

Bryan and Jeremy met in 1988, when Bryan was born and Jeremy told his parents to "take him back." Believe it or not, Bryan is actually two years younger than Jeremy despite the height and beard growth differential. They have overcome bouts of hostility, both verbal and physical, over the years to become close today. Whether attending Orioles games, hitting the ski slopes, or working at Camp Oest, they are frequent companions in misadventure. Bryan was the first to inform Meaghan that she was accepted into the family since they took her crab picking, the Maryland symbol of acceptance.

Tom Ports

Tom and Jeremy met in the summer of 2003 at Camp Oest when Jeremy offered to help a bowl-cut-sporting Tom with dripping candle wax into a bin of sawdust to create fire starters. They became good friends over four summers working together at Camp Oest and many events outside of camp. Whether painting the blue trail or getting a drink at Harvey's Jr., conversations inevitably turn to reminiscing about camp. They share a healthy appreciation for grilling, Natty Boh, and Teva sandals. Tom often allowed Jeremy to call Meaghan on his cell phone while away at camp, but this often led to Tom talking with Meaghan more than she talked with Jeremy. These conversations allowed Tom and Meaghan to become "like this".
